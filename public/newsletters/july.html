Welcome to the Happy July 2013 Newsletter!
 
 
Why so 'Happy' you ask? Well, if this is the July newsletter, that means August isn't very far away! And since August is basically only good for preparing for Halloween, you can almost taste the fun size Snickers and zombie face paint in the air.

And why so Hummingbird? Well, that's the name of the website sending you this newsletter. The same website whose logo you've been scheming to tattoo somewhere on your body in a fit of rebellious teen angst. Hey, we know how it is, we're totally cool.

And what will we be discussing in this month's newsletter? Bonus points if you've already figured it out from the Subject Line. Wait, you're not even going to try and guess?

Too bad, you get no Bonus points. You get Boner points. Boner in the olden-timer-g-rated sense of the word, you know, "to mess up."

The theme of this email is to cover the significant changes that have happened on Hummingbird over the last couple of months. If you're a long time member, we love you for that. If you've received your invitation to join our beta and haven't accepted it yet, you'll probably find your name in our Death Note by the end of the month.

Let's start things off by having a look at our brand spanking new logo!
 
 
In the 1920s, this would be considered porn.
 
 
Our old logo was probably the worst thing in America. It's a good thing you hesitated on that tattoo. This new logo however, is sleek and mysterious. Like a beautiful bullet that can also shit on your windshield. This is the new face of Hummingbird. We hope you like it as much as we do, and if you don't? Well, that's just how we weed people out of our lives.
 
 
 
 
Calling All Mad Scientists
 
 
The Organization has us cornered. We've developed the Hummingbird API but we need your help to create amazing apps. This is the choice of Steins;Gate. It's still a work in progress but it'll be ready for you within the next two weeks. You can of course start playing around with it now as well as commenting on functionality you'd like to see. You'll need a Lab Member number, hurry up and register your Hummingbird Account
 
 
 
 
A New Way to Explore
 
 
Our old Explore page was nothing more than a pile of useless anime thumbnails. Nobody cared about that, not even us. After spilling countless warm combinations of Red Bull and desperation across our keyboards over the course of many sleepless nights, we are finally able to bring you an Explore page that focuses more heavily on anime discovery by highlighting trending anime based on your favorite genres, the latest reviews and of course, trending reviews. We've also added MUCH more sophisticated anime filtering which will continue to improve as we move forward.
 
 
 
 
Freaking Activity Feeds
 
 
What have you been watching lately? What have your friends been watching lately? Hm... let's check our amazing activity feeds that we released for just that purpose as well as uh... sharing adorable videos of kittens meeting hedgehogs to the soundtrack of Toy Story with each other.
 
 
 
 
Anime, In a Different Light
 
 
Our original anime page lacked a lot of content and everything was big... bigger than Christmas. So, the internet sentenced us to be murdered to death by means of mostly mispelled slurs. But hey, this is the internet, where just about every third website dies if they are killed. Fortunately, we didn't. We've revamped our anime pages to not only be better designed but also contain more useful content, including places to legally watch (coming soon!) and buy the anime. You can thank us later.
