Oh My! It's The Terrifying October 2013 Newsletter!
 
 
I've got mostly bad news, but some good news. Consider this newsletter the shitty box of raisins in your bag of candy. Hell, maybe I'll even ruin your favorite holiday, at least a little bit.

I'll start with the bad news…This newsletter is 30 days late. To make that seem less bad, here's more bad news: Halloween won't always be the joy-filled evening of snack-sized Snickers and girls dressed as Sexy Naruto that it should be. As you leave the trick-or-treating stage of your life behind and enter the alcohol-and-crazy-penis-costumes phase of Halloween, you tend to care less about the candy and more about the partying. Unfortunately, Halloween parties tend to be pretty much the same as your regular Friday nights, except everyone is wearing a dumber outfit than usual.


Whew, enough with the bad news. I'm bumming myself out. The good news is, we're unveiling something very exciting: a different, limited-run anime shirt every month designed by a kick-ass t-shirt artist. We're calling it the ‘Shirt of the Month’ (SotM). Pretty darn clever, right?

Quality, fashion-forward shirts with art printed at a retail level; rather than using a rubbery plastic to apply the art to the shirt, the shirt is garment dyed. You can't pick it off. You won't be able to feel the art on your shirt and the art won't crack or crumble over time, as most screenprinted shirts do. It will last as long as the shirt does.

The biggest reason we're doing this is to provide a way for the community (That's you!) to help support us financially and get something kick-ass and pajama-comfortable in the process. Did we mention each shirt will increase the likelihood of sexy women (or men, depending on your preference) kissing you right on your anime-loving mouth?

Here's a preview of November's SotM. It's on sale two days early so you can go ahead and order that beautiful crap right now. We've included a size chart on the product page so you don't end up looking like a package of hot dogs, or a kite.

"But Josh," you're probably saying (in that Walter White voice you've been working on) "I don't like Pokemon! Maybe I'll buy next month’s shirt…"

Honestly, if you don't buy a shirt, Vik and I won't lose sleep or spend our nights weeping softly, but we may have to resort to more ads on the site or some other sort of prostitution. Do you really want to see Vik wearing a mini-skirt? Actually, don't answer that. You should be ashamed.

As for the extensive redesign we've been working on behind the scenes, I promise it's still coming along! We know some of you haven't visited the site in a while due to your studying for an astronaut exam or saving starving children, but feel free to check out the first stage of the redesign we've rolled out, the new and improved Hummingbird forums.

Now then, go claim your Shirt of the Month. For those of you that do buy a shirt, thank you. You're helping us continue doing what we love doing, making Hummingbird awesome. You can thank us later for all the sexy lady (or dude) kisses.

If you enjoyed the newsletter, be sure to tweet and let us know or discuss it on the forums, or we'll send Shinigami Mcdonald after you!
